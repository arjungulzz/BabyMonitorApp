Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Baby Monitor App (the "Application") for mobile devices, created by apadev232228. This service is intended for use for monitoring infants within a local home network environment.

1. Information We Collect (Local Only)

The Application requires access to your device's Camera and Microphone to function. This access is used solely to stream live audio and video to other devices within your own local Wi-Fi network.

  • No Cloud Storage: Audio and video data are streamed directly peer-to-peer over your local network.
  • No Server Uploads: We do not upload, record, or store any of your audio or video data on external servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Required for application distribution and security.
  • AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including your device's advertising ID and limited location data to provide relevant ads.
  • Google Play Billing: Used for processing Pro version upgrades. Payment information is handled exclusively by Google; we do not store your credit card or billing details.

3. Local Network Usage

The Application acts as a local MJPEG server. Data transmitted over your Wi-Fi network is unencrypted by default (HTTP). We recommend using a password-protected, private home Wi-Fi network to ensure your local stream is secure from unauthorized local access.

4. Data Retention

We do not collect or retain personal data on our servers. Any settings or preferences are stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the application or clearing the app cache.

5. Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to our processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us.
